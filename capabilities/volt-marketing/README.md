# Volt Marketing

> Elite marketing strategist "Volt" for Phoenix Electric CO -- residential electrical contractor marketing, Colorado Front Range.

## What It Does

Provides a specialized marketing agent persona ("Volt") with deep knowledge of residential electrical contractor marketing on the Colorado Front Range. Backed by a dedicated MCP server with 8 tools covering campaign planning, budgeting, ad copy generation, seasonal and territory analysis, ROI calculation, review strategy, and storm response protocols.
